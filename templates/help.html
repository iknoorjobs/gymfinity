This is how to use 'Gymfinity':

For New users-->

  1. Fill the form which is obtained when you click on REGISTER button in the navigation bar.
  2. Click on Create Account button.
  3. After filling form make sure you confirm your email adresss while verifying you email.


For Existing Users-->

  1. Fill your username or Membership number (which is unique).
  2. Fill password of your Account.
  3. Click on Login button.


Who can become a partner?

Person owning a gym , who is or having a well reputed trainer and experience as well can become a part of 'Gymfinity'.It is open for everybody having a gym.
